Bio

Hi! I’m Ben 👋

I’m a software engineer specializing in audio and graphics stuff 🧑‍💻. I’ve got experience making desktop audio applications and plugins for use in music production 🎛️. I also worked in the broadcasting industry making augmented reality telestration software used in sports broadcasting contexts ⚽.

I worked for 5 years as a secondary maths teacher. I taught maths and further maths GCSE and A-Level at schools in south and central London 🧑‍🏫.

I have a long deejaying career stretching back to my teenage years 🎧. I have deejayed at many clubs and festivals across the UK. I’m the proud cofounder of the Midnight Groovies club night 🪩. I’m the cofounder of the Community Transmission radio station 📻. I am a member of the Flatline producer group 📉. These days I mainly spin 90s classics at weddings and birthday parties 🥳.

I am the proud father of two beautiful boys 💕.
